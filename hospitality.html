What is Kimpton Hotel?

Kimpton Hotel is a stylish, boutique hotel located in the heart of Washington, DC, near Dupont Circle. Renowned for its modern design, vibrant atmosphere, and personalized service, the hotel offers luxurious accommodations with contemporary decor and upscale amenities. Guests can enjoy features like a rooftop pool with stunning city views, an on-site restaurant serving innovative cuisine, and complimentary perks such as wine hours and loaner bikes. Its prime location provides easy access to iconic attractions like the White House, National Mall, and local dining and shopping options, making it a perfect choice for both leisure and business travelers.

What is The Faena Hotel Miami Beach?

The Faena Hotel Miami Beach is a luxurious, oceanfront retreat that blends art, design, and world-class hospitality. Located in Miami’s vibrant Mid-Beach neighborhood, the hotel is renowned for its glamorous Art Deco-inspired architecture and its bold, eclectic interiors curated by designer Alan Faena. Guests can enjoy stunning ocean views, spacious rooms and suites, and amenities like the award-winning Tierra Santa Healing House spa, an opulent pool area, and direct beach access. The property also boasts exceptional dining, including the celebrated Los Fuegos by Francis Mallmann and Pao by Paul Qui, and is home to the Faena Theater, offering dazzling live performances. Perfect for those seeking an upscale, artistic, and unforgettable Miami experience.

What is Hotel Emma?

Hotel Emma, located in the heart of San Antonio's historic Pearl District, is a luxurious boutique hotel celebrated for its rich history, distinctive design, and exceptional hospitality. Originally built in 1894 as a brewhouse, the hotel seamlessly blends industrial charm with modern elegance. Guests can enjoy world-class dining at Supper, a curated library in the hotel's lobby, and easy access to Pearl’s vibrant dining, shopping, and cultural scene. Perfect for discerning travelers, Hotel Emma offers a uniquely Texan experience steeped in heritage and sophistication.

What is the Acqualina Resort & Spa?

Acqualina Resort & Spa, is a luxurious oceanfront retreat located in Sunny Isles Beach, Florida. Renowned for its Mediterranean-inspired design, impeccable service, and stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean, the resort offers spacious suites and rooms, each elegantly appointed with top-tier amenities. Guests can indulge in world-class dining, relax at the award-winning Acqualina Spa, or unwind by one of the three oceanfront swimming pools. With direct beach access, a kids’ program, and personalized experiences, Acqualina is ideal for both family getaways and romantic escapes. It’s consistently ranked among the top luxury resorts in the world.

What is San Ysidro Ranch?

San Ysidro Ranch is a luxurious and historic boutique resort nestled in the foothills of Santa Barbara, California. Known for its charming cottages, lush gardens, and sweeping views of the Pacific Ocean and Santa Ynez Mountains, the property offers a serene and romantic escape. Each private cottage is exquisitely designed with modern comforts, fireplaces, and outdoor spaces, including hot tubs and stone patios. The ranch features award-winning dining at The Stonehouse and the Plow & Angel, renowned for their farm-to-table cuisine and intimate ambiance. With a history dating back to the 19th century and a legacy of hosting celebrities and dignitaries, San Ysidro Ranch embodies timeless elegance and tranquility.

What is Hotel Wailea?

Hotel Wailea, within the lush hillside of Wailea, Hotel Wailea is an adults-only luxury resort offering an intimate and serene escape on Maui's stunning coastline. This Relais & Châteaux property features 72 spacious one-bedroom suites, each with elegant furnishings and private lanais overlooking tropical gardens or the ocean. Guests can indulge in farm-to-table dining at The Restaurant at Hotel Wailea, enjoy exclusive beach services, or explore the island with curated experiences like outrigger canoeing or private chef-led cooking classes. With its tranquil ambiance and breathtaking views, Hotel Wailea is perfect for couples seeking romance and relaxation.
